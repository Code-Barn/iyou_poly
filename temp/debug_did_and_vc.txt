Here’s a **summary of our efforts** so far, along with the **current state** and **next steps** to continue later:

---

## **1. Problem Summary**
### **Goal**
- Enable users to **generate a DID and VC** on demand via the **VC management page**.
- Ensure the **VC management page updates** to display the **generated VC**.

### **Issues Encountered**
1. **VC Issuance Failing**:
   - The **`issue_vc`** function fails with the error **`Failed to issue VC: (1, 'key expansion failed')`**.
   - This occurs because the **`key`** passed to **`didkit.issueCredential`** is not in the correct format.

2. **HTMX Request Not Reaching the View**:
   - The **`GenerateDIDAndVCView`** is not being called by the **HTMX request**, even though the **CSRF token** and **URL** are correct.

3. **Browser Not Updating**:
   - The **VC management page** does not update after clicking the **`Generate DID & VC`** button, even when the **VC is generated**.

---

## **2. Debugging Efforts**
### **2.1. VC Issuance**
#### **Root Cause**
- The **`key`** passed to **`didkit.issueCredential`** must be a **JWK string**, but the format was incorrect.
- The **`user.did_key`** is already a **JWK string**, but the **`issue_vc`** function still fails.

#### **Fixes Attempted**
1. **Pass the `key` as a JWK string**:
   - Updated the **`GenerateDIDAndVCView`** to pass **`user.did_key`** directly to **`issue_vc`**.
2. **Log the `key`**:
   - Added **detailed logging** to verify the **`key`** format.

#### **Current State**
- The **`user.did_key`** is a **valid JWK string**, but the **`issue_vc`** function still fails.
- The **`didkit.issueCredential`** function might expect a **specific format** for the **`key`**.

---
### **2.2. HTMX Request**
#### **Root Cause**
- The **HTMX request** is not reaching the **`GenerateDIDAndVCView`**, even though:
  - The **CSRF token** is included in the **request headers**.
  - The **`hx-get`** URL is correct.
  - The **user is authenticated**.

#### **Fixes Attempted**
1. **Verify CSRF Token**:
   - Ensured the **CSRF token** is included in the **HTMX request headers**.
2. **Debug the Request**:
   - Added **logging** to verify the **request headers** and **method**.

#### **Current State**
- The **HTMX request** is still not reaching the **`GenerateDIDAndVCView`**.
- The **server logs** do not show the **`GenerateDIDAndVCView`** being called.

---
### **2.3. Browser Not Updating**
#### **Root Cause**
- The **VC management page** does not update because:
  - The **HTMX response** is not being rendered correctly.
  - The **`vc_container.html`** partial template is returned, but **HTMX is not swapping it** into the DOM.

#### **Fixes Attempted**
1. **Verify HTMX Attributes**:
   - Ensured the **`hx-target`** and **`hx-swap`** attributes are correct.
2. **Manually Trigger the View**:
   - Used **`fetch`** to manually trigger the **`GenerateDIDAndVCView`** and verify the response.

#### **Current State**
- The **manual `fetch` request** returns the same **HTML content** as the **current page**.
- The **browser is not updating** even when the **VC is generated**.

---
