How DID Authentication Works

  1. Your Verifiable Credential contains your identity information signed by a trusted issuer
  2. We verify the cryptographic signature to ensure the credential is authentic
  3. We check that the issuer is trusted (or in open trust mode, any issuer is accepted)
  4. If valid, you're logged in securely without needing a password

Security Note: Never share your private keys. Only share your Verifiable Credentials.
